In accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), we collect your name, shipping address, and contact details exclusively for order fulfillment. We do not sell your data. In compliance with the CPA, all electronic goods carry a 6-month implied warranty against factory defects. Should a product fail under normal use, it may be returned for replacement or refund. Unused products in their original, sealed packaging may be returned within 14 days of receipt.
